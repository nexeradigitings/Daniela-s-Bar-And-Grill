01 — The Spot

A real neighborhood bar,
not a stage set.

Tucked into Thompson Lane, Daniela's is the kind of place where the bartender knows your order before you sit down. Neon in the windows, graffiti-painted specials on the wall, and a jukebox that leans hard into Mexican music alongside classic dive-bar staples.

It's unfussy, it's loud when it needs to be, and it's been a fixture for the folks who live nearby. Come for happy hour, stay for whoever's playing that night.
